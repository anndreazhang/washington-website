About Us

A Patient-Centered Approach to Anesthesia Care

At Washington Anesthesia Partners, our top priority is delivering exceptional anesthesia services to our patients with a focus on excellence and a patient-centric approach.

Our team comprises CRNAs and anesthesiologists providing services in physician offices and outpatient surgery centers, emphasizing patient comfort and safety throughout every procedure — and positive patient outcomes above all.

Our Services

Comprehensive Anesthesia Practice Management

We provide end-to-end operational support so that your anesthesia practice runs smoothly — from scheduling and staffing to billing, compliance, and beyond.

Scheduling

We use data analysis to track case duration trends and staffing needs, with centralized coordination designed to prevent conflicts and streamline resource allocation across your facility.

Recruiting

We attract and retain qualified providers by emphasizing competitive compensation, professional development opportunities, and a work-life balance — supported by a strong online presence and professional networking.

Billing

Precise coding, prompt post-procedure billing, and diligent claims management — paired with ongoing staff training on billing updates to avoid fines and audits.

Inventory Management

Just-in-time supply systems, volume purchasing for better pricing, and structured protocols for disposing of expired supplies — keeping your operation efficient and waste-free.

Quality Assurance

Strict assessment and monitoring protocols grounded in evidence-based guidelines, with a focus on provider competence, continuing education, and consistent communication across care teams.

Compliance

A multifaceted approach to regulatory compliance: clear internal guidelines, regular training, smart technology implementation, accountability structures, and ongoing team communication.
